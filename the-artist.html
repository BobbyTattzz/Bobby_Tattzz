The Artist

Bobby “DaMaddTatter” Arocho

Founder of Exclusive Artz LLC, Bobby is a multidisciplinary artist blending tattoo craftsmanship, 3D modeling, digital illustration, painting, and street-style creativity into one unified artistic identity.

Each piece Bobby creates reflects a life of hustle, discipline, imagination, and raw storytelling. From skin to canvas, from digital realms to physical builds — everything is crafted with intention and driven by innovation.

Inspired by graffiti culture, sci-fi energy, spiritual exploration, and mechanical imagination, his work transforms ideas into reality. Whether it's tattoos, murals, 3D prints, digital animations, or apparel — every project is handled with mastery and passion.

Skills & Specialties

  • Custom Tattoos (Black & Grey, Color, Graffiti, Realism)
  • 3D Printing & Modeling (STLs, RC parts, characters, props)
  • Digital Art, Logos, Character Design
  • Murals, Wall Art, Large-Scale Paint Projects
  • Clothing Designs & Custom Apparel
  • Graffiti Lettering • Neon Aesthetics • Tech/Cosmic Art
